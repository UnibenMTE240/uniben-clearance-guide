Await Clearance Review by Officer

After uploading your documents, your clearance request is sent to the assigned clearance officer for review.

Step 1: Status Changes to "Clearance Requested"

Once you submit your documents, the Kofa portal will update to show Clearance Requested. This means your documents are now being reviewed by the clearance officer.

Step 2: Officer Reviews Your Submission

Your clearance officer will manually go through each document to confirm that they are correct, properly scanned, and match your details.

Step 3: If Approved ‚Äì Status Changes to "Cleared"

If all your documents are accepted, your Kofa status will change to Cleared. You can now proceed to physical clearance and registration.

Step 4: If Rejected ‚Äì Status Changes to "Clearance Started"

If there's an issue (e.g. wrong file, blurry image, wrong document), your clearance will be rejected. The system will show the reason, and your status will return to Clearance Started.

Always check your portal daily during this period. Fix and re-upload any rejected document promptly to avoid delay.
