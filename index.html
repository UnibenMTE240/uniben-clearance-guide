Await Clearance Review by Officer

After uploading your documents, your clearance request is sent to the assigned clearance officer for review.

Step 1: Status Changes to "Clearance Requested"

Once you submit your documents and request clearance, the Kofa portal will update to show Clearance Requested. This means your documents are now being reviewed by the clearance officer.

Step 2: Officer Reviews Your Submission

Your clearance officer will manually go through each document to confirm that they are correct, properly scanned, and match your details.

Step 3: If Approved ‚Äì Status Changes to "Cleared"

If all your documents are accepted, your Kofa status will change to Cleared. You can now proceed to school fee payment.

Step 4: If Rejected ‚Äì Status Changes to "Clearance Started"

If there's an issue (e.g. wrong file, blurry image, wrong document), your clearance will be rejected. The system will show the reason, and your status will return to Clearance Started.

Always check your portal daily during this period. Fix and re-upload any rejected document promptly to avoid delay.

Course Registration in UNIBEN

After completing your clearance, the next step is to register your courses. UNIBEN uses two types of course registration: physical course registration and online course registration. You must complete both.

Important: Do not register random courses. Always confirm your course list from your department or academic adviser.

Physical Course Registration (Comes First)

This is the first step. It involves physically going to the department of each course you are offering to register for that course. Here‚Äôs how it works:

  • Make a list of all the courses you are expected to take for the semester.
  • Visit each department offering the courses (including electives).
  • A certain amount is usually paid to each department for registration.
  • Keep any receipt they issue.

Online Course Registration (Kofa Portal)

After completing the physical registration, head to the UNIBEN Kofa portal to register those same courses online:

  • Login to your Kofa account.
  • Go to ‚ÄúMy Data‚Äù ‚Üí ‚ÄúStudy Course.‚Äù
  • Select the current session and semester.
  • Register the exact courses approved by your department.
  • Submit and print your course registration slip.

Note: You need to pay your school fees before online course registration is enabled.

Both physical and online registration must be completed to fully register your courses. Skipping one can cause problems during exams or result processing.

Your courses are now officially registered. If you made a mistake, visit your department early to correct it before deadlines.
